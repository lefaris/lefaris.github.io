This is an introductory undergraduate course in the field of Robotics. This course introduces robotic systems, 
covering essential robotic system components and application implementation. Students participate in hands-on 
lessons about basic robotic components, sensors, control systems, visual recognition, and other emerging 
technologies. Students in EEL 1603 implement theories applied onto the Hiwonder PuppyPi ROS quadrupeds.
